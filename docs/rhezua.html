Rhezua Okila
"Rhezua Okila. A name you may know, though I advise against assuming you understand its weight. I lend my talents to the study of Air Mysth, not idle gossip. If you seek favor, come with leverage. If you seek debate, come prepared. And if you mistake my patience for pliancy? That would be ... unwise."

– Rhezua
